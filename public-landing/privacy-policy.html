Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zamanak ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our calendar synchronization service at hamgam.zamanak24.ir. This public information page is hosted at zamanak24.ir for Google OAuth verification.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Zamanak, we may collect:

  • Google Account information — your email address, obtained through Google OAuth, to identify your connected account.
  • Google Calendar data — calendar events created or updated by Zamanak, including event titles, descriptions, dates, and colors you configure.
  • App settings — your preferences such as which fields to include in calendar event descriptions, event color, and vacation sync options. These are stored securely on our servers linked to your account.
  • OAuth tokens — Google refresh and access tokens required to interact with Google Calendar on your behalf. Tokens are stored securely on our servers; only short-lived session data may be cached in your browser during use.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Connect your Google Calendar account to Zamanak
  • Create, read, and update calendar events related to your appointments
  • Display your connection status and saved preferences
  • Maintain and improve the App's functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or Google user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Google API Services

Zamanak's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We request the Google Calendar scope (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events) to create, update, and manage calendar events related to user appointments.
  • Google user data is used only to provide calendar synchronization features.
  • We do not use Google user data for serving advertisements.
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google data unless required for security, legal compliance, or with your explicit consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

Google OAuth refresh tokens, your connected Google account email, and sync preferences are stored securely on our servers. OAuth token exchange is performed server-side over HTTPS. Your browser may temporarily cache a session access token during active use. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time using the "Disconnect" button in the App, which removes stored Google tokens from our servers, or by revoking Zamanak's access from your Google Account permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

Zamanak is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@zamanak24.ir
